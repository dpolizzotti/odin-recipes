Cheesecake Recipe

Cheesecake with Fruit

This recipe is easy to make and only requires basic ingrediants and attention to complete!

It's best to ensure you follow the ingrediants exactly as described to get the most tasty and creamycheesecake!

Ingrediants

  • Creamcheese
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla

Cooking Instructions

  1. Whip the cream cheese and sugar together in large bowl
  2. Add eggs in one at a time while whipping cream cheese and sugar
  3. Add vanila after you have added all the eggs
  4. Once combined, put into oven 350 for one hour!

ENJOY!
