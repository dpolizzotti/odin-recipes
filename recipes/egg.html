The Ultimate Egg Sandwich

  • Eggs
  • English Muffin
  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  1. Warm pan to high, grease it with butter and crack two large eggs into center
  2. Split English Muffin and flip eggs
  3. Flip the eggs after a brief cook time
  4. Cook Bacon and place cheese on muffin
  5. Remove the eggs, place onto Muffin with bacon and cheese
  6. Eat and Enjoy
