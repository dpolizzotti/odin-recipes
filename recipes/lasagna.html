Meat Lasagna Recipe

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Sause
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Spices and Seasonings to Taste
  1. Pre Heat Oven to 400 Degrees
  2. Place Lasagna Noodles down inside dish pan
    • Ensure pan is well sprayed with grease agent to prevent sticking
  3. Layer Noodles and Meat Sauce back and forth, interlocking layers as you go
  4. Place in over after you've made the entire dish
  5. Cook for 30 minutes
  6. Remove and Enjoy!
