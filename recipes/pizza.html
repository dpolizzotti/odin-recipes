Sonja's Stupdenous Pizza!

How to make the best pizza on the planet

While basic, this recipe is one of the best and cheesiest pizza recipes ever

Just follow these instructions and you'll be rich in having sold slice after slice of delicious heavenly goodness

Ingrediants

  • Cheese
  • Dough
  • Green Peppers

Cooking Instructions

  1. Flatten out dough
  2. Layer cheese onto dough with green peppers
  3. Put into oven at 450 for 20 minutes

enjoy!
